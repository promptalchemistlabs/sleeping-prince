## Start with the experience

Your customer is not only buying a glass vessel and plants. They are buying a guided, calming experience and the confidence to care for what they made.

## Build your floor price

List every material, including breakage and leftover stock. Add venue cost, preparation, facilitation time, payment fees and a 15% contingency. This is your floor—not yet your selling price.

## Pay yourself for invisible work

Two workshop hours can require four more hours of sourcing, packing, travel and follow-up. Price the whole job.

## Test a simple package

Offer one clear vessel size, a stated duration and one optional upgrade. You will learn faster from a simple offer than from six confusing tiers.
