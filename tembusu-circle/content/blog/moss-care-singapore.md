## Bright, indirect light

Place the terrarium where the plants receive steady daylight without direct afternoon sun. Glass can trap heat quickly.

## Read the condensation

A light mist that clears during the day is useful feedback. Constant heavy droplets usually mean the system needs less water or more air exchange.

## Observe before intervening

Closed terrariums reward patience. Change one variable at a time and give the living system several days to respond.
