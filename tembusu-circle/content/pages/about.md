## Why Tembusu

The tembusu is a hardy native tree: slow-growing, deeply rooted and able to flourish through change. That is the kind of business we want our members to build.

## What we share

We turn hard-won operating lessons into clear playbooks—from keeping moss alive in Singapore's climate to pricing a 20-person corporate workshop.

## Who belongs here

Makers testing their first paid workshop, studio owners building repeatable systems and curious people deciding whether terrariums could become more than a hobby.
